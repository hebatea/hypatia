Privacy Policy
Last updated: 8 May 2026

What we collect

  • Your Telegram user ID and first name
  • Your daily check-in answers
  • Your step work answers (Steps 1, 2, 3)
  • Your reminder time and timezone
  • Date and time of each entry

What we do not collect

  • Your phone number
  • Your full name
  • Payment information
  • Your location beyond timezone

Where your data is stored

Your data is stored in PostgreSQL hosted by Supabase and the bot runs on Railway. Both use encrypted storage in secure data centres.

Who can see your data

Only the operator of this bot. Your answers are never shared with third parties, never used for advertising, and never sold.

How long we keep it

Your data is kept for as long as you use the bot. Delete everything at any time with /deletedata in the bot. Deletion is immediate and permanent.

AI processing

Your check-in and step answers are sent to Anthropic's API to generate a personalised reflection. Your answers are not used to train AI models.

Your rights

Access your data with /myhistory.

Delete your data with /deletedata.
